# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Crypto degen traders and early alpha hunters scanning Bankr for newly deployed tokens. They live in Discord, Telegram, and X — not Bloomberg terminals. They want speed and signal, not noise. Context: checking dashboard between trades, often on desktop with dark mode, high ambient distraction.

## Product Purpose

NyxScout is a real-time scanner that detects newly deployed Bankr tokens and scores them on a 0-100 alpha scale using 8 legitimacy signals. It surfaces the tokens most likely to moon before the crowd notices. The dashboard is the primary interface — a live feed, not a historical archive.

## Brand Personality

**Dark, sleek, dev-native.** Think Linear.app meets Vercel precision. Geometric confidence, not mystical theatrics. The dashboard is a precision instrument — calm, fast, trustworthy. The night goddess metaphor is quiet sophistication, not gothic decoration.

3 words: **Precise. Unhurried. Inevitable.**

Emotional goal: the user opens NyxScout and feels *in control* — not overwhelmed by data, not chasing hype, but standing above the noise with a sharp instrument.

## Anti-references

- **Not luxury-mystical**: no gold gradients, no ornate serifs, no "elite" posturing
- **Not Bloomberg-brutalist**: no dense terminal grids, no tabular data vomit, no 8px fonts
- **Not the current dashboard**: the 320px fixed sidebar + card grid is cramped and generic. Redesign from zero
- **Not crypto-gamer**: no neon green on black, no glitch effects, no meme energy
- **Not SaaS-cream**: obviously

## Design Principles

1. **Signal, not noise.** Every element must earn its place. If it doesn't help the trader decide, cut it.
2. **Calm speed.** Fast to scan, unhurried to read. Information density through hierarchy, not compaction.
3. **Depth through reduction.** The fewer elements, the more each one means. Negative space is a feature, not waste.
4. **Linear precision.** Geometric, intentional, measured. Every pixel justified. Like a well-tuned IDE.
5. **Dark by nature, not by genre.** The dark theme serves the night-scout metaphor, not crypto cliché.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for all text and interactive elements
- Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against surface backgrounds
- Keyboard-navigable token feed with visible focus rings
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all animations
- No information conveyed by color alone (score badges also show numeric value)
